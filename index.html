Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How long does delivery take? +

We deliver within 3–5 business days across Bangladesh. Express delivery options are also available at checkout.

How do I use a coupon code? +

Open the cart, enter your coupon code in the Coupon field and click Add. Try SMART10 for 10% off your order!

Can I return a product? +

Yes, we offer a 7-day return policy on all eligible items. Contact us within 7 days of delivery to initiate a return.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and cash on delivery across all areas of Bangladesh.
